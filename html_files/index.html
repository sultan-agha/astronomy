    What Is Astro Belts?


    Astro Belts is a free, web-based collection of digital interactives and videos highlighting
    the latest developments in astronomy and Earth science.

    Astro Belts gives you the opportunity to explore our planet, solar system, galaxy, and universe.
    Astro Belts is developed by a team of Computer Science students from the Lebanese University
    who collaborate to ensure that content is accurate, up-to-date, engaging, relevant, and accessible to a wide audience.


    Interactives

    Astro Belts interactives allow you to explore objects and materials from different perspectives, discovering how we
    can combine information to better understand the universe.



    Different forms of light:
    Explore visible and invisible wavelengths of light that help
    us understand features like the dusty brim of the Sombrero Galaxy
    roughly 30 million light-years away.

    Hidden objects:
    Unveil invisible light to reveal hidden objects like the stars
    forming inside Mystic Mountain, a pillar of gas
    and dust 7,500 light-years from Earth.
